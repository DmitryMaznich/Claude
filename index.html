Kako uporabljati Smart Wash? How to use Smart Wash?

Enostavna navodila za uporabo samopostrežne pralnice Smart Wash. Simple instructions for using Smart Wash self-service laundromat.

1
Naložite perilo v stroj Load laundry into machine
Odprite vrata in naložite perilo. Najboljša kakovost pranja je pri približno polovični napolnitvi bobna. Ne prenapolnite - perilo mora imeti prostor za gibanje. Open the door and load laundry. Best washing quality at approximately half-full drum. Don't overload - laundry needs space to move.
2
Izberite program na zaslonu Select program on screen
Pritisnite gumbe na nadzorni plošči stroja. Izberite temperaturo (30°, 40°, 60° ali 90°). Cikel pranja traja približno 40 minut. Press buttons on machine control panel. Choose temperature (30°, 40°, 60° or 90°). Washing cycle takes approximately 40 minutes.
3
Vstavite žetone in pritisnite START Insert tokens and press START
10 kg stroj = 5 žetonov • 18 kg stroj = 8 žetonov. Vstavite žetone v režo. Pritisnite gumb START za zagon. Detergent in mehčalec se dodajata samodejno. 10 kg machine = 5 tokens • 18 kg machine = 8 tokens. Insert tokens into slot. Press START button to begin. Detergent and softener are added automatically.

Preverite etikete na oblačilih Check garment care labels
Pred pranjem vedno preverite navodila na etiketah oblačil. Upoštevajte priporočene temperature. Izberite temperaturo glede na vrsto tkanine. Always check care labels before washing. Follow recommended temperatures. Choose temperature according to fabric type.
👟
Ne perite in sušite obutve Don't wash or dry shoes
Strogo prepovedano: pranje in sušenje obutve v strojih lahko poškoduje stroje in vašo obutev. Za čiščenje obutve uporabite specializirane storitve. Strictly prohibited: washing and drying shoes in machines can damage both machines and your footwear. Use specialized services for shoe cleaning.
🔍
Izpraznite žepe Empty all pockets
Pred pranjem odstranite vse predmete iz žepov: ključe, kovance, papirnate robčke, telefone. Zaprite zadrge, gumbe in kavlje, da preprečite poškodbe. Remove all items from pockets before washing: keys, coins, tissues, phones. Close zippers, buttons and hooks to prevent damage.
🎨
Ločite belo in barvno perilo Separate whites and colors
Vedno ločite belo perilo od temnega in barvnega. Nove barvne obleke perite ločeno prvič, ker lahko spustijo barvo. To prepreči barvanje svetlega perila. Always separate white laundry from dark and colored items. Wash new colored garments separately first time as they may bleed. This prevents color transfer to light items.
⏱️
Ne puščajte perila v strojih Don't leave laundry unattended
Po končanem ciklu takoj odstranite perilo iz stroja. Perilo, puščeno v strojih, lahko postane vlažno in zasmradi. Omogočite tudi drugim strankam uporabo strojev. Remove laundry immediately after cycle completes. Laundry left in machines can become damp and develop odors. Also allow other customers to use machines.
🚫
Prepovedano pranje Prohibited items
Ne perite: predmete z motornimi olji ali bencinom, zelo umazane delovne obleke s kemikalijami, velike preproge, živalske kože. Za take predmete uporabite specializirane čistilnice. Do not wash: items with motor oils or gasoline, heavily soiled work clothes with chemicals, large carpets, animal hides. Use specialized cleaners for such items.
⚠️
Pomembno: Odgovornost Important: Liability
Smart Wash ne odgovarja za izgubljene, ukradene ali poškodovane predmete. Ne puščajte dragocenosti v žepih. Preverite stroje in okolico preden zapustite prostor. Uporaba storitev je na lastno odgovornost. Smart Wash is not responsible for lost, stolen or damaged items. Don't leave valuables in pockets. Check machines and surroundings before leaving. Use of services is at your own risk.
