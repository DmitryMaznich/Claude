**Q: How often is the laundromat cleaned?**
A: Every day.

**Q: How are the washing machine drums disinfected?**
A: They are disinfected during daily cleaning using a special disinfectant. Additionally, a special disinfecting agent is automatically added to the drum during every wash cycle to destroy all harmful bacteria.

## üêæ Pets & Specific Items
**Q: Can I wash pet items (bedding, rugs, clothes)?**
A: It is not desirable to wash pet items in the regular machines. However, complete control over this is difficult. Therefore, if you are concerned about hygiene, we recommend always using the disinfectant spray (which is available at the laundromat) to spray the drum before loading your laundry.

**Q: Can I wash shoes?**
A: NO. Washing shoes in our machines is strictly PROHIBITED. Shoes can damage the drum and the heavy tumbling can damage the shoes themselves.

## üß¥ Detergents
**Q: Can I turn off the automatic detergent supply? I want to use my own.**
A: NO, it is not possible. Although theoretically possible, the system is integrated and automatic. We do not allow disabling it.

**Q: Can I add my own detergent?**
A: It is not recommended. Our machines automatically dispense professional detergent, softener, and disinfectant. Adding your own may cause oversudsing and affect the wash quality.

**Q: What kind of detergents do you use? Are they hypoallergenic?**
A: We use high-quality commercial detergents from various manufacturers (Slavic and Italian). They are typically hypoallergenic and have a neutral scent (no strong perfume).

## üß∫ Laundry Etiquette & Usage
**Q: What if a machine has finished but the owner isn't there, and I need to wash?**
A: It is the owner's responsibility to retrieve their laundry on time. If a cycle has finished and the owner is absent, you may carefully remove their items, place them in a laundry basket or on a clean surface with reasonable care, and use the machine.

**Q: Is it safe to leave my laundry in the washer?**
A: YES. The washing machine locks the door for the entire duration of the program (approx. 40 minutes). No one can open it or take your items while the cycle is running.

**Q: I can't open the dryer door! Is it locked?**
A: NO. Dryers use strong magnets, not locks. They do not lock even during operation (so you can check dryness). If it feels stuck, simply pull harder.
